# Product Guidelines: JADE 2026 Website

## 1. Tone and Style

The website's content and design will maintain a **Professional and Informative** tone. This approach emphasizes credibility, expertise, and precision, aligning with the serious nature of a dental exhibition while providing clear and factual information to the audience.

## 2. Visual Aesthetic and Brand Identity

The visual aesthetic will be **Elegant and Sophisticated**. This choice aims to convey prestige, quality, and a high standard, reflecting positively on both JADE 2026 and WKCI as hosts. The design will be refined, utilizing a polished and professional presentation to underscore the event's importance and the stature of its participants.

## 3. Key Messaging Priorities

The core messaging for the website will integrate three main priorities:

*   **Highlighting Expertise and Innovation in Dentistry:** Content will focus on showcasing the latest advancements, research, and best practices within the dental field, positioning JADE 2026 as a leading platform for knowledge exchange.
*   **Emphasizing Networking Opportunities and Industry Growth:** The website will promote the event as a crucial hub for professionals to connect, collaborate, and explore new business opportunities, fostering growth within the dental industry.
*   **Showcasing Global Reach and Diverse Participation:** Information will highlight the international scope of JADE 2026, featuring diverse speakers, exhibitors, and attendees from around the world to attract a broad international audience.

## 4. Visual Content Guidelines

All visual content on the website will adhere to the following guidelines:

*   **High-Resolution and Professional Imagery Only:** Only high-quality, professional photographs and graphics will be used to maintain the elegant and sophisticated aesthetic, ensuring a premium visual experience.
*   **Incorporation of Promotional and Informational Videos:** A strategic mix of videos will be utilized to enhance engagement. This includes promotional videos to build excitement for the event and informational videos to provide deeper insights into exhibition highlights, speaker topics, and WKCI's role.
*   **Optimization for Fast Loading and Mobile Responsiveness:** All images and videos will be meticulously optimized to ensure rapid loading times and seamless display across all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones, thereby guaranteeing an excellent user experience for all visitors.

## 5. Content Accessibility and Inclusivity

To ensure the website is accessible to the widest possible audience, the following guidelines will be observed:

*   **Adherence to WCAG 2.1 AA Standards:** All content and functionality will be developed in compliance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, addressing diverse needs including visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments.
*   **Use of Clear, Concise Language and Avoidance of Jargon:** Content will be written in plain language, easy to understand, and free from unnecessary technical jargon. When specialized terms are unavoidable, they will be clearly explained to ensure comprehension for all users.
